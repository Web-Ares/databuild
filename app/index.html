LOCATION HAINAUT

The Portfolio Databuild Invest includes six retail parks located south west of Belgium in the province of Hainaut, in the Walloon region. The province has a surface area of 3,800 km² and a population of 1.3 million inhabitants. The largest cities are Mons (the capital of Hainaut) and Charleroi (the fifth largest city in the country by population). Hainaut’s citizen average income per inhabitant is of 15,003 EUR/year, higher than the Brussels average of 13,525 EUR/year.

Accessibility

The main motorways leading to Hainaut include the E403 and E17 from Ghent and the North West, the E19 from Brussels and the E42 from the East side of Belgium.

Location of Databuild Invest retail parks

The province of Hainaut shares its borders with the French North department. Three of the six retail parks are located close to the French border. A considerable part of the catchment area of these parks therefore includes French customers. The nearest French cities are Lille, Valenciennes and Maubeuge.
